Nikola Tesla

The person who invented electricity

Here's time line of Nikola Tesla life:

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Kroasia.
  • 1870 - Graduate in engineering and physics.
  • 1880 - Working in the electrical industry.
  • 1888 - Invention of the induction motor.
  • 1890 - Developing wireless lighting and wireless power distribution.
  • 1943 - Dies at the age of 86.
